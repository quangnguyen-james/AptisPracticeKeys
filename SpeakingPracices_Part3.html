Test 1: In this part I’m going to ask you to compare to pictures and I will then ask you two questions about them. You will have 45 seconds for each response.

1. Tell me what you see from two pictures?
2. Which place do you like more? pine forests or beaches?
3. Does the weather affect people’s emotions? Why? 		1. Looking at the two pictures, I can see people doing different activities in different places.
In the first picture, there are 2 people standing next to a snow-covered pine forest.
The place looks peaceful and green, and the atmosphere is very quiet.
In the second picture, I can see 2 people sitting and watching the sea in the sunset.
They look relaxed and happy. The beach looks bright and open.
The two pictures have a clear difference in atmosphere, in picture 1 the air is very cold while in picture 2 it is very warm
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. I prefer the beach. Living in Ho Chi Minh, I like taking my children to the sea because they can run and play in the sand.
A beach trip is easy to organize, and everyone can relax in the sun.
Also, the open space helps me forget work for a while and enjoy family time.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Yes, the weather often affects mood.
Sunny days usually make people feel happier and more energetic — they want to go out and meet friends.
On the other hand, long rainy or grey days can make some people feel tired or a bit low, although some find rain calm and good for thinking.
In short, weather changes activities, and activities influence feelings.
Test 2: In this part I’m going to ask you to compare to pictures and I will then ask you two questions about them. You will have 45 seconds for each response.

1. Tell me what you see in the two pictures?
2. What's so attractive about these two places?
3. How to protect natural beauty? 		1. Looking at the two pictures, I can see people doing different activities in different places.
In the first picture, there is a group of people climbing a mountain, they are lined up in a very disciplined line.
In front of them are snow-capped mountains.
In the second picture, there is a scene of a beach, it looks like it is an island.
The scene is quite quiet, with only a few people.
The two pictures have a clear difference in atmosphere, in picture 1 the atmosphere seems cold but dynamic, while picture 2 is warm and peaceful.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Both places are attractive because they give a break from city life.
The mountain place is attractive for its fresh air, shade and quiet — it helps me think and relax.
The beach or open area is attractive because it is bright, we can play, and there is space to walk.
Personally, I like any places where I can relax and recharge.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. We can protect natural beauty with small, everyday actions.
People should not drop litter, follow local rules, and avoid destroying plants.
Governments and communities can make rules, provide bins and run clean-up campaigns.
If everyone does a little, nature stays beautiful for the next generation.
