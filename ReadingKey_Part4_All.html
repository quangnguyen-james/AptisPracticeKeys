Subject: Volunteering to clean the local park

No. Question (Câu hỏi) Quote (Trích dẫn)
1 Who thinks the park is a beautiful place to relax? "I adore the gorgeous scenery and fresh air at the park."
2 Who can't help because of busy life? "I just have too much work that I don't have much free time for myself."
3 Who will ask other people to help? "I will ask my husband to help out since he usually doesn't do much anyway."
4 Who thinks cleaning should happen regularly? "The community should work together to organize cleaning events more often."
5 Who thinks volunteering is important for children? "Cleaning the local park not only helps keep the environment clean but also teaches kids the value of teamwork and dedication.".
6 Who thinks other local areas need cleaning? "This is a reminder that not just the park, but other areas in our town also need regular cleaning.".
7 Who thinks volunteering can help with future employment? "I believe these experiences will benefit them in the future, helping them sharpen skills needed for their future careers.".

Subject: the comments section of an online magazine article about flying on airplanes.

No. Question (Câu hỏi) Quote (Trích dẫn)
1 Who thinks that flying is a bad experience? "I think that flying is a bad experience overall, with long security lines and cramped seats."
2 Who visits relatives regularly? "My son and his family live far from me, so I visit relatives regularly by plane"
3 Who needs to fly for their work? "People find it strange that I dislike flying, especially as a travel blogger who frequently needs to fly for my work."
4 Who wants to work in other countries? "While I want to work as a tour guide in other countries to share my love for travel with others."
5 Who suggests increasing the flight ticket price? "I even suggest increasing the flight ticket price to reduce the number of flights.".
6 Who tries to protect the environment? "I usually walk or cycle to local grocery stores instead of driving, and I limit my use of plastic as much as possible.".
7 Who liked relaxing while traveling? "For me, visiting vineyards and connecting with local producers was not just about business; it was a chance to unwind and appreciate the beautiful landscapes".

Subject: the comments section of an online magazine article about eating.

No. Question (Câu hỏi) Quote (Trích dẫn)

3 Who prefers to eat home-cooked food? "Fast food is nowhere near as good as home-cooked food because it feels healthier and more comforting than eating out"

5 Who only eats a few types of food? "However, I just eat a few types of food, sticking to simple, familiar dishes that I know I'll enjoy.".
6 Who is taking a cookery course? "I'm also taking a cookery course to improve my skills, so I can recreate some of my favorite meals at home".
7 Who prefers to eat alone? "Now, I prefer to eat alone, where I can enjoy my meal in peace without distractions".
