HUIT B2 Aptis - Speaking Pactices-Part 1

In this part, I am going to ask you three short questions about yourself and your interests. You will have 30 seconds to reply to each question.
1. What did you do yesterday?
2. Please tell me about your friends.
3. Please tell me about your favorite film.
Speech (Lời nói) Audio listener
1. Yesterday I worked at my office in the morning.
I had some meetings with my team because we are developing a new project.
In the evening, I spent time with my children. We played together, and I helped them with their homework.
After that, I relaxed by watching TV before going to bed.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. I have many friends, but the closest ones are my classmates from my master’s program.
We are from different ages, so our group is very interesting.
We often go out for coffee, have meals, or even sing karaoke together.
They make me feel happy and relaxed after work.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. My favorite movie is Red Rain. I like it because it is interesting and meaningful.
The story is about the history of Vietnam and the soldiers who fought to protect Quang Tri citadel.
I like the special effects and the good coordination between the characters.
I watched this movie with my group of friends, and we all enjoyed it.
In this part, I am going to ask you three short questions about yourself and your interests. You will have 30 seconds to reply to each question.
1. Please tell me about your favorite place.
2. Please tell me about your favorite film star.
3. Please tell me about the last time you saw an advertisement.
Speech (Lời nói) Audio listener
1. My favorite place is Nha Trang city in Vietnam.
I like it because it has both beautiful beaches and mountains.
The people are friendly and the food is delicious, especially seafood.
I went there with my family last summer, and we had a great time together.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. My favorite film star is Jackie Chan.
I like him because he is funny and also very talented in action movies.
I enjoy watching his films with my children, and we always laugh a lot.
He is also a good example of hard work and energy.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. The last time I saw an advertisement was on TV. It was about a mobile phone.
The ad showed the new features like a better camera and a long battery life.
I really like it but I do not want to change my phone in this time.
I often use phones for both work and study.
In this part, I am going to ask you three short questions about yourself and your interests. You will have 30 seconds to reply to each question.
1. Please tell me about your typical day.
2. What sports do people in your country often play?
3. Please tell me about your favorite place.
Speech (Lời nói) Audio listener
1. On a typical day, I go to work in the morning. I have meetings with my team and check the progress of our software projects.
In the afternoon, I continue working or studying for my master’s program.
In the evening, I spend time with my children. We play together or I help them with their homework.
After that, I relax a little and then go to bed.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. In Vietnam, the most popular sport is football.
People of all ages like to play it, especially in the evenings or at weekends.
Many people also enjoy badminton because it is easy to play in small places.
Some young people go to the gym or play basketball, but football is still number one.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. My favorite place is Ho Chi Minh City because it is where I live and work.
The city is very busy and modern, with many coffee shops and restaurants.
I enjoy going out with my friends to drink coffee or eat together.
Even though the traffic is crowded, I still love this city because it has many opportunities and energy.
