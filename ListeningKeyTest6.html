Two professional educators are talking about university. Read the opinions below and decide whose opinion matches the statements, the man, the woman, or both the man and the woman. You can listen to the discussion twice.

No. Questions (Câu hỏi) Answers (Trả lời) Quote (Trích dẫn)
1 The internet makes education more accessible [Both] "The internet has made education more accessible."
2 Social interactions are essential to university life [Man] "Social interactions are a crucial part of university life."
