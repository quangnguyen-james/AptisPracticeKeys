1. Describe the picture
2. Tell me there is the last time you went shopping.
3. Why do some people dislike busy places? 		1. In the picture, I can see a shopping street.
There are not many people walking; some of them are carrying bags, and others are looking into shop windows.
The place doesn't look crowded but seems full of energy. I think it could be outside of a shopping mall.
The weather seems bright and clear, which makes the atmosphere more lively.
Overall, it looks like a popular place for shopping and meeting friends.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. The last time I went shopping was about two weeks ago.
I went with my children to the Go supermarket near my house.
We needed to buy some clothes for our children and also some groceries for the week.
After shopping, we decided to have lunch in a small restaurant inside the mall.
It was a little crowded, but we still enjoyed the time together.
Shopping can be tiring, but it is also a good way to spend time with family.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. I think some people dislike busy places because they feel uncomfortable with too much noise and too many people around.
It can be stressful to wait in long lines or to move slowly in the crowd.
Some people prefer quiet places where they can relax and enjoy peace.
Also, busy areas can sometimes be hot or unsafe, especially if there are pickpockets.
Personally, I don’t mind going to crowded places sometimes, but I prefer calm areas when I feel tired or need to focus.

1. Describe the picture
2. Tell me about the last time you traveled with a group?
3. What are the benefits of traveling with a group? 		1. In the picture I can see several people sitting on a bus.
The bus is not very crowded and most of the passengers look young.
One woman is wearing headphones and smiling brightly.
Some people are looking out of the window and enjoying the view.
A few passengers seem to be sleeping or resting. The overall atmosphere looks calm and relaxed.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. The last time I went on a group trip was last month with my master students to Binh Duong.
We took a taxi and talked a lot during the trip, making the trip feel short.
To prepare for this trip, 2 girls prepared a lot of food for the whole group, they always did their best.
When we arrived, we arranged the place and the necessary things for grilling food, we grilled together, bathed in the stream, ate the prepared food and joked around while bathing.
It was a very fun and relaxing weekend, bringing us closer.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Traveling with a group has many advantages.
It usually saves money because people can share transport and accommodation costs.
It also feels safer and more comfortable when you are with friends.
Group trips are more enjoyable since you can share experiences, take photos together, and support each other.
Planning becomes easier because responsibilities are divided among the group.
For me, group travel creates good memories and improve friendships.

Picture (hình) | Questions (Câu hỏi) Speech (Lời nói) | Audio listener

1. Describe the picture
2. Do you prefer reading newspapers or watching news?
3. Why do people need to watch news? 		1. In the picture there are two people standing close together. A woman who looks like a reporter is holding a microphone.
She is interviewing a man who is wearing a suit and tie. Both of them are dressed smartly and look professional.
The background is plain, so the focus is on the interview.
The scene gives a calm and serious feeling and makes me feel curious about the topic they are discussing.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. I prefer watching news on TV or online because it is faster and easier to follow.
I'm very busy because of working and studying, I often do not have much time to read long articles.
Short video clips or headlines give me quick updates I can watch between tasks.
However, when I need more details for work or my master's study, I sometimes read longer articles.
So I use both, but watching news fits my daily routine better.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. People need to watch the news to know what is happening locally and around the world.
News helps us make daily decisions, for example about travel, safety, or work plans.
It also keeps citizens informed about politics and social issues, which is important for participating in society.
For working, news helps us update new things related to our work and give useful information.
Finally, watching the news helps people feel connected to the wider world.
