Part 3 Question 15 - Câu hỏi nam nữ

- Đối với đáp án [Both] thì dù có đảo hội thoại thì vẫn đúng - Nên học thuộc
- Đối với đáp án [Man]/[Woman], muốn chắc thì nên thuộc trích dẫn

A man and a woman are talking about information technology. Read the opinions below and decide whose opinion matches the statements, the man, the woman, or both the man and the woman. You can listen to the discussion twice.

No. Questions (Câu hỏi) Answers (Trả lời) Quote (Trích dẫn)
1 Future generations fail to cope with technology information. [Man] "Future generations might really struggle with all the information technology throws at them."
2 Technology revolution is good for the economy. [Woman] This technological revolution is beneficial for our economy."
3 No computer is superior to the human brain. [Woman] "No matter how advanced they get, computers can't replicate the complexity of the human brain."
4 More should be done to protect individual privacy. [Both] "Protecting privacy is essential in this digital age."
