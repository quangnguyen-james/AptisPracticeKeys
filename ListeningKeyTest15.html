Four people are talking about when they like listening to music. Complete the sentences below.
No. Answers (Trả lời) Quote (Trích dẫn)
1 [Speaker A] To relax "I like to listen to music when I want to relax. It helps me switch off from everything."
2 [Speaker B] While studying "I usually pick something without lyrics, like instrumental or electronic music, so it doesn't interrupt my thinking."
3 [Speaker C] While singing "I like to sing along and try to improve my voice. It's a fun hobby."
4 [Speaker D] After waking up "I like to start my day with music as soon as I wake up. It helps me feel energized."

Part 3 Question 15 - Câu hỏi nam nữ

Các bạn lưu ý đi thi người ta có thể đảo đoạn hội thoại dẫn tới đảo đáp án. Nên khi đi thì các bạn kết hợp nghe nữa nhé!
- Đối với đáp án [Both] thì dù có đảo hội thoại thì vẫn đúng - Nên học thuộc
- Đối với đáp án [Man]/[Woman], muốn chắc thì nên thuộc trích dẫn

Listen to two people discussing singers and music. Read the opinions below and decide whose opinion matches the statements, the man, the woman, or both the man and the woman. You can listen to the discussion twice.

No. Questions (Câu hỏi) Answers (Trả lời) Quote (Trích dẫn)
1 Singer can be good models for the young. [Woman] "I really believe that singers can be good role models for young people. Artists like Taylor Swift or Ed Sheeran often promote kindness and hard work."
2 Taste in music is a highly personal thing. [Both] "What resonates with one person might not resonate with another."
3 Music is a universal language. [Man] "I believe it's a universal language that connects people from different cultures."
4 Music can be used to manipulate people's feelings [Both] "Music can be used to manipulate people's feelings, whether in advertising or emotional moments in movies."
